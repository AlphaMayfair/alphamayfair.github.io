About Alpha Mayfair

Alpha Mayfair is a London-based nightlife host and concierge service specialising in exclusive Mayfair venues. From VIP tables and bottle shows to curated guestlists and private celebrations, we connect you with the city’s most in-demand clubs and events.

Our focus is simple: smooth entry, premium hosting and unforgettable nights in London’s most iconic nightlife district.

What We Do

  • VIP table reservations at Mayfair’s top clubs (Cirque, Reign, Tape, TABŪ, Cuckoo & more)
  • Girls’ guestlists and promoter tables with complimentary drinks in selected venues
  • Birthday, celebration and group night planning
  • Advice on the best club for your date, music taste and group size

Our Venues

We regularly host across a curated selection of Mayfair and central London hotspots including: Cirque Le Soir, Reign Showclub, Tape London, TABŪ, The Cuckoo Club, Dear Darling, INCA / Luna, Selene and Luxx Club.

How It Works

  • Send us your date, preferred club and group size via WhatsApp or the booking forms.
  • We confirm availability, table options and minimum spends (if applicable).
  • On the night, you arrive to a hosted experience – with smooth entry and guidance.
